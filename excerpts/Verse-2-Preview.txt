Kaale shisheyan de pichhe feeling the rush,
Sadkan te nishaan chhad-da breaking the hush.
Pahiya ghumda ae tezz gripping the wheel,
Tera jhootha pyaar saara I know the deal.
Vairi mere sarhde ne seeing my pace,
Khoon naal likhi si main winning the race.
Dhokha tu kitta ae playing the game,
Munda main ziddi haan claiming my name.
