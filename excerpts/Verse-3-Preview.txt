Lohe da engine gaje lighting the spark,
Gusse vich turda haan owning the dark.
Tu sochi main rul jaunga falling so low,
Par main taan uth khadna watch me glow.
Tera dhokha ae sajna I paid the price,
Hun mera dil pathar cold as ice.
Pichhe mud ke na dekhan leaving the past,
Mera nawa roop dekhi built to last.
