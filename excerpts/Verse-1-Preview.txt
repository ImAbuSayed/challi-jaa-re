Kaali rati nikalda haan under the moonlight,
Khayalan vich tu hi vasdi losing the fight.
Thandi hawa te teri khushbu driving me crazy,
Kach de pyale vich my vision gets hazy.
Tere nakhre da shikaar falling down slowly,
Munda main azaad haan living so boldly.
Puchhda haan taareyan ton what is the reason,
Tu mainu chhad gyi in this cold season.
