Challi jaa re challi jaa breaking my heart,
Kidaan main tainu bhulava tearing apart.
Teri yaadan mainu aawan making me blind,
Tere wargi koi hor hard to find.
Challi jaa re challi jaa walking away,
Dil mera tod ke nothing to say.
Sajna main rulda haan lost in the night,
Tu mainu chhad ditta far from my sight.
